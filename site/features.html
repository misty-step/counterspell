features

Keep your Fable sessions on Fable.

Counterspell watches every Claude Code session that has ever run claude-fable-5 and puts it back the moment it drifts — automatically, before your turn ends.

Stay on Fable Your sessions stay on the model you chose
No wrong window It always acts on the right window
Fix mid-turn A downgrade reverses before the turn even finishes
Hands off Your deliberate model choices stay untouched

how it works

One Rust binary reads your Claude transcripts and your live window state on a schedule — no background daemon of its own.
